The Best Movies According to Venky

My top 3 movies of all-time

Bahubali

Strong Characters: Prabhas, as Baahubali, and Rana Daggubati, as Bhallaladeva, deliver memorable performances that anchor the film's emotional and dramatic arcs.

Animal

Dark and Intense: Animal explores themes of crime and vengeance with a gritty and dark narrative, showcasing Ranbir Kapoor in a more intense and violent role.

RRR

Spectacular Visuals: RRR is a visual extravaganza, with breathtaking action sequences and impressive CGI that enhance the epic storytelling.
